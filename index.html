About SAMEBA

The South African Maintenance and Estate Beneficiaries Association (SAMEBA) is a political movement dedicated to advocating for the rights of maintenance and estate beneficiaries across South Africa.

Our mission is to ensure fair treatment, legal representation, and economic empowerment for all beneficiaries through policy reform, education, and community engagement.
