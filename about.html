About SAMEBA

Our history, mission, and vision for South Africa

Our History

Founded in 2018, SAMEBA emerged from the need to address systemic issues faced by maintenance and estate beneficiaries across South Africa. Our founders recognized the lack of political representation for this important segment of society.

Starting as a small community organization in Limpopo, we have grown into a provincial movement with thousands of members advocating for change.

Our Mission

SAMEBA exists to protect and promote the rights of maintenance and estate beneficiaries through:

  • Policy advocacy and legal reforms
  • Community education and empowerment
  • Economic development initiatives
  • Political representation

We believe in creating sustainable solutions that uplift beneficiaries and their families.

Our Vision

We envision a South Africa where:

  • Every beneficiary receives fair and timely maintenance payments
  • Estate distribution is equitable and transparent
  • Beneficiaries have access to education and economic opportunities
  • The rights of vulnerable groups are protected by law

Our Core Values

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our dealings.

Community

We prioritize collective wellbeing and solidarity among beneficiaries.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in service delivery and representation.
